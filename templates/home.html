We make apps

Cornell App Development is a project team at Cornell University dedicated to mobile app development

Every year we take a new app from idea to finished product, culminating with a release on the App Store.
View our past work

Our team is made up of talented designers and developers who collaborate to bring projects to life.

We give students the opportunity to work in a startup environment and gain practical experience in software development.

Want to learn how to make iOS apps?

Not everyone involved with our team is a coding expert. Every semester we run a 10 week developer in training program for Cornell Students interested in learning about iOS development. Over 300 students applied last year and less than 10% of them had prior experience developing for the platform.
Learn more about our training program

Have an idea?

We are always on the lookout for great app ideas to bring to life. If you think you have a killer idea, submit a proposal. If we choose it, you'll get credit on our site for your stroke of genius!
